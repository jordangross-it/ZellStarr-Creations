Description

Welcome to Landgraab Landing, a bar for Oasis Springs that is, in my head, owned by the Landgraab family, specifically Malcom. It has themes of space as well as a collection of artifacts. Here, your sims can enjoy the the best that the Oasis Springs bar scene has to offer: luxury, enjoyment, and artistic-vigor.

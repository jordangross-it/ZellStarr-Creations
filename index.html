About

I’ve been building in The Sims 4 since 2015, exploring a variety of design styles from contemporary to castles for aliens. Every detail, from plants to accent walls, is part of the fun! Huge thanks to my inspirations—Deligracy, Lilsimsie, and James Turner—and to everyone who supports my gallery posts.

Playstyle

Since the beginning of my simming journey, I have always played with a chaotic-neutral style, I would say. While my sims generally remain prosperous and determined in their goals... sure some mayhem may ensue. Whether it be collecting gravestones to making fires.
